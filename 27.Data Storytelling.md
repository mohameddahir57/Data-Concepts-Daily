##  What Is Data Storytelling?

**Data Storytelling** is the practice of combining:

1. **Data** (facts, numbers, analysis)
2. **Visuals** (charts, dashboards, graphs)
3. **Narrative** (clear explanation and context)

The goal is simple:

> Help people understand *what is happening*, *why it matters*, and *what to do next*.

##  Why Numbers Alone Are Not Enough

Look at this number:

> **Sales dropped by 12%.**

That number alone raises many questions:

* Why did sales drop?
* When did it start?
* Which products were affected?
* Is this bad or expected?
* What should we do?

Data storytelling answers those questions.
